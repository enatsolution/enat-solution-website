Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our recruitment services

How long does the hiring process take?

Our average placement time is 2 weeks from initial consultation to candidate placement. For urgent positions, we can often deliver qualified candidates within 3-5 business days.

What support do you offer post-placement?

We provide 24/7 ongoing support including 90-day placement guarantees, regular check-ins, and assistance with any integration challenges to ensure long-term success.

Do you work with remote positions?

Yes! We specialize in remote, hybrid, and on-site placements across all our industries. Our network includes candidates comfortable with various work arrangements.

What industries do you specialize in?

We focus on three key areas: Information Technology (software development, cybersecurity, data analysis), Healthcare (nursing, medical administration), and General Staffing (administrative, customer service, operations).
